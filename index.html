2018

August 2018

Sounding Cities project: A collaborative project between the cities of Birmingham (UK) and Johannesburg kicks off in Johannesburg with Clare Loveday, saxophonist Naomi Sullivan, clarinettist Luke Newby and artist Nandipha Mntambo at The Centre for the Less Good Idea. City Deep for Bb clarinet and alto and baritone saxophone was written for this project. Further concerts in Johannesburg at Wits University in August and in Birmingham, UK, in October 2018.

Clarinet Trio 1 for clarinet, violin and piano, Klein Karoo Klassique. Morne van Heerden (clarinet), Waldo Alexander (violin), Mareli Stolp (piano).

Saxophone Octet 3, World Saxophone Congress in Zagreb, Croatia. Saxophone Octet of the Royal Birmingham Conservatoire, directed by Naomi Sullivan.
